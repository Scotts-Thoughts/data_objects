Scrafty (Japanese: ズルズキン Zuruzukin) is a dual-type Dark/Fighting Pokémon introduced in Generation V.

It evolves from Scraggy starting at level 39.

Scrafty can Mega Evolve into Mega Scrafty using the Scraftinite. Mega Scrafty was introduced in Pokémon Legends: Z-A.

Biology

Scrafty is a bipedal, lizard-like Pokémon. It has oval, wide-set eyes with small pupils and gray eyelids, as well as small nostrils and a wide mouth with bared teeth, giving it the appearance of a permanent sneer. It sports a red crest on its head, similar in appearance to a mohawk. Baggy yellow skin hangs off its shoulders, falling halfway down its back. As shown in the animated series, it can wear this skin like a hood. Scrafty's body is orange, with thin arms and a gray, scaled belly. Its lower body and tail are covered by loose yellow skin resembling baggy trousers. On its tail is a single red scale.

Scrafty is powerful enough to kick through concrete blocks used by Conkeldurr, and can also spit acidic liquid from its mouth, which it does to taunt opponents. It can protect itself while attacking by pulling up or folding the loose skin around its waist. Scrafty lives in groups with other members of its species. Groups determine hierarchy by crest size, with the Scrafty having the largest crest becoming the leader. The larger the crest, the more arrogantly Scrafty acts. A group will attack anything that comes within its territory. Scrafty never leaves its territory for a long time. Though often coming off as rude, it takes good care of its loved ones and its turf. Scrafty never leaves the territory it watches over its whole life long.

Forms

Scrafty can Mega Evolve into Mega Scrafty.

The shed skins of Mega Scrafty turn from a yellowish color to pure white thanks to Mega Evolution, which also causes the skins to toughen and become more resilient. The shed skins are worn by Mega Scrafty in two parts. The top part is worn over its head and shoulders akin to a hoodie, which splits into multiple trailing strips at the back. The hood retains the eye holes present in its base form, but it is not seen using it to cover its face. The backside of Mega Scrafty's crest sticks out of the skin, resembling a ponytail. The bottom part is worn by Mega Scrafty like a pair of pants, now including V-shaped holes on the knees; unlike regular Scrafty, Mega Scrafty's "pants" do not cover its tail. Mega Scrafty also adopts a slouching, imposing stance in battle, cracking its neck when it Mega Evolves. It remains as feisty as ever.

In animation

Main series

Major appearances

Scrafty debuted in Meowth's Scrafty Tactics!. It was kicked out of its den by a wild Mandibuzz, who herself had been forced to relocate. Scrafty begged a wild Beartic, Scolipede, and Darmanitan to help it win back its territory, but none of them helped it. As a result, it resorted to kidnapping Iris's Axew and holding him ransom. Meowth and Ash's Scraggy negotiated with and distracted Scrafty whilst Ash, Pikachu, and Snivy rescued Axew. Later, Scrafty confronted Mandibuzz, only to be defeated. However, Scraggy, using the High Jump Kick he had learned from Scrafty, defeated Mandibuzz and won Scrafty's den back.

  • Mega Scrafty is the only Pokémon with a base stat total of 588.
  • Mega Scrafty is tied with Wo-Chien for the highest base Special Defense of all Dark-type Pokémon.

Origin

Scrafty appears to be based on a lizard molting its skin. Its crest shares some similarities with the elongated scales of iguanas.

Scrafty seems to also be based on stereotypes of urban youth subcultures, sometimes seen as delinquent or confrontational. Its sagging "pants" and "hoodie" seems to reference the hip hop subculture, while its bright red crest resembles a mohawk hairstyle from the punk subculture. The hood also doubles as a balaclava, a face covering commonly used as a disguise during crimes.

Mega Scrafty's shed skin turning white, along with it using said skin as a "hoodie", might allude to popular pictures of leopard geckos shedding their skin all at once, making them look as if they were wearing a whitish hoodie or onesie.

Name origin

Scrafty may be a combination of scrappy (feisty), scruffy, or scraggy (scrawny), and crafty (being skilled at deceit to achieve one's goals).

Zuruzukin may be a combination of 狡 zuru (sly fellow), 狡い zurui (crafty), or ずるずる zuruzuru (onomatopoeia for dragging slowly) and 頭巾 zukin (hood) or possibly skin.
